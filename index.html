Cat's range is the real story. Lucky cat realism. A full San Judas Tadeo sleeve. Anime portraits with sharp color. A photorealistic dart frog. She does not have one genre — she has one standard: it has to be undeniable when it's finished.

        
She built Tu Madre's around the idea that a tattoo studio should feel like a place you want to be. Warm without being precious. Technical without being cold. Her energy in the chair matches her work: focused, generous, and relentlessly detail-oriented.

        
"There's no such thing as a tattoo too small or too weird. Just bring the idea."
